Our 10-Point Humanization Criteria

How our algorithm ensures your text reads exactly like a human wrote it.

1. Sentence Variety

Mixes short, punchy sentences with longer, complex ones to create a natural, engaging reading rhythm.

2. Natural Transitions

Replaces rigid, robotic connectors (like "Furthermore") with seamless, conversational transitions.

3. Conversational Tone

Uses simple, real-world language that feels approachable rather than overly academic.

4. Personal Touch

Weaves in subtle opinions, real-world examples, and small details reflecting genuine human thought.

5. Embracing Imperfection

Avoids the "too-perfect" structure of AI, retaining the raw, authentic feel of human writing.

6. Dynamic Structure

Actively avoids repetitive paragraph structures and list formats that often trigger AI detectors.

7. Natural Contractions

Properly utilizes everyday contractions (it's, don't, can't) to mimic natural speech patterns.

8. Context Awareness

Adapts the flow based on the text's actual meaning, entirely avoiding plug-and-play templates.

9. Unpredictable Phrasing

Removes common AI clichés and highly predictable word pairings in favor of creative phrasing.

10. Emotional Resonance

Introduces relatable elements and subtle emotional undertones that truly resonate with readers.
